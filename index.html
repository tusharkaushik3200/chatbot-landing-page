Process

Upload Documentation

Upload documentation pdf or your documentation website link our engine will capture all nested information and train the LLM model with your data.

01
A reservoir of Intelligence
Our engine creates knowledgebase from the docuementation / website/ 3rd party mediums.
02
Structuring the Universe of Data
Organize your raw data into categories, entities, and relationships, and structures the content so it reflects a hierarchy of understanding. This scaffolding becomes the blueprint for seamless retrieval and augmentation.
03
Generate Response
At the heart of the KB lies a powerful retriever. Whether using traditional keyword-based search or advanced neural retrieval models, the system should provide instant and relevant responses. This step makes your Knowledge Base not just accessible but indispensable.
04
Output Delivery
Design interfaces that are simple yet powerful, offering users a clear path to discovery. Whether through a chatbot, web interface, or API, the experience should feel like a conversation with an expert
